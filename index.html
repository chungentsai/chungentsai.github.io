About

Welcome to my website! My name is Chung-En. I'm a research assistant in computer science at National Taiwan University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yen-Huan Li and to collaborate with Prof. Hao-Chung Cheng since 2021. Previously, I have worked with Prof. Chun-Hsiung Hsia and Prof. Kai-Min Chung. I received my bachelor's degree in computer science from National Taiwan University in 2023.

I am broadly interested in machine learning theory, mathematical optimization, and computational complexity theory. I am currently working in online learning and large-scale optimization with the logarithmic loss.
